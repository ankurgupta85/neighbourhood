Neighbourhood is a friendly network of people who are willing to interact with their neighbours. If you are moving to a new area and would like to interact with your neighbours to befriend them before knocking their door, this is the best application you can use.

1) Add Neighbours
2) Search Neighbours - Zipcode, Username and Name
3) Invite our Neighbours to join your network
4) Send Messages to Neighbours
5) Upload your Images
6) Create Events
7) Invite your Neighbours to Events
